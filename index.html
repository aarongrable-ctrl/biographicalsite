Welcome to my website!

My name is Aaron Grable and I am a graphic design and web design teacher at El Camino High School. I like anything having to do with nature because I grew up in Oregon but now call Fallbrook my home. I was in the Navy and I now have two old English sheepdogs named Henry and Murphy.

I’m Aaron Grable, a CTE graphic design and web design teacher at El Camino High School in Oceanside, CA. I help North County San Diego students build college- and career-ready portfolios through project-based learning, real-world client work, and industry tools like Adobe Creative Cloud, HTML/CSS, and WordPress. My classes articulate with MiraCosta College for potential credit, and I prioritize inclusive, accessible instruction and community partnerships so every student can publish professional work they’re proud to share.
